Part-Time Position

Locations: Remote Anywhere

Rebase Ventures is a permanent equity venture studio, focused on building and buying SaaS companies. We are expanding our acquisitions this year, focusing primarily on buying micro-SaaS developer tools and API products. We are looking for an ambitious part-time associate to join the acquisitions team, working remotely from anywhere in the world.

This is an ideal role for anyone early in their career or still studying, who wants to build a career in technology VC or PE. The weekly hours are flexible, and we will work around your other commitments where needed.

You will be helping with all aspects of the private equity acquisitions process. From sourcing potential companies, to evaluation, duediligence and transaction. We are a small tight knit team, and you will be working directly with the founder Damien Tanner on a daily basis.

Given our target acquisition space, you should have a interest and understanding of technology, SaaS and APIs. Programming skills are not required, but certainly are positive.

Required Skills

  • Interest in technology, and how the modern web is built
  • Excellent written and spoken English
  • Expert Excel skills
  • Understanding of company financing and accounts
  • Experience analyzing company metrics, especially SaaS metrics

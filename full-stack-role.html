Full-Time Position

Location: Cairo

Join the core development team in our Cairo office. Learn from the best, and become a master developer.

You will be working full-time as part of the developemt team. Everyone in the team is a full-stack developer, although each person has their individual focus areas. If you're coming from a front or backend developer position, that's okay, we'll help you learn the new skills to transition to full-stack.

We are a Venture Studio, so we work on multiple software products. This means the technology can be different between products. You should enjoy learning new technology, and be comfortable switching between languages and frameworks. Today, we mainly use React.js and Node.js. In the future, we're likely to also use Rails and Django.

We work using an agile project management process. We have daily standups, weekly sprint planning and retrospectives. GitHub Issues is used for backlog management. We do regular pair programming and code reviews.

You should be abitious and have a hunger to learn new things. If you have a drive to rapidly advance your skills and career, this is an ideal role for you.

Required Skills

  • 2+ years experience working as a front-end or backend-engineer
  • Excellent written and spoken English
  • A technical degree from a leading university
  • Experience with React.js and Node.js
  • Understanding of MySQL/PostgreSQL

Optional Additional Skills

  • Experience with other backend frameworks like Rails or Django
  • GraphQL knowledge
  • AWS experience (EC2, S3, Lambda)

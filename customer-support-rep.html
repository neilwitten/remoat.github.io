Full-Time Position

Location: Cairo

We are looking for ambitious Customer Support Representatives to join our Cairo office (although currently during Covid-19 we are all working safely from home).

You should be ambitious and have a hunger to learn new things. If you have a drive to rapidly advance your skills and career, this is an ideal role for you.

Required Skills

  • 2+ years experience working in a customer support role with offshore accounts
  • Very good written and spoken English
  • Ideally some experience writing customer support material (FAQs, knowledge base articles)
  • Proficient in software tools like Google Docs, Spreadsheets, support software.

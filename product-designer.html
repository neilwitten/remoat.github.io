Full-Time Position

Locations: Cairo or Remote Anywhere

Rebase Ventures is a global venture studio. We incubate and grow our own B2B SaaS products. We are looking for a product designer with experience designing SaaS applications to join our Cairo office (currently working from home due to Covid-19) or work remotely from anywhere in the world.

You will be working full-time as part of the Rebase Ventures internal product team. You will work closely with the founder Damien Tanner (previously co-founder of 4 successful SaaS tech companies including Pusher).

We work using an agile project management process. We have daily standups, weekly sprint planning and retrospectives.

You should be ambitious and have a hunger to learn new things. If you have a drive to rapidly advance your skills and career, this is an ideal role for you.

Required Skills

  • Experience designing user interfaces for web applications
  • A love for simple and easy to use UI
  • Experience using modern design tools like Figma
  • Proficient in HTML and CSS
  • Excellent written and spoken English

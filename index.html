We build and acquire SaaS

Rebase Ventures is a venture studio that incubates and buys software companies.

Acquisition

We acquire B2B SaaS companies. We strive to make the acquisition process fair and simple. Our goal is to grow the products we acquire for the long term. We consider companies in all shapes and sizes, selling to any industry.

Incubation

We incubate our own products. Our focus is SaaS in industries where the competition is lackluster and customers are deserve a better product. We believe great design and user experience are absolutely key to success.

About Us

Rebase Ventures is founded by serial entrepreneur and investor Damien Tanner.
